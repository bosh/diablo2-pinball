For windows:

In admin CMD shell run:

mklink /d "C:\PATH\TO\diablo-pinball\gmc\addons\mpf-gmc" "C:\PATH\TO\mpf-gmc\addons\mpf-gmc"

For unix:

sudo ln -s PATH/TO/mpf-gmc/addons/mpf-gmc PATH/TO/diablo-pinball/addons/mpf-gmc

See:
https://missionpinball.org/latest/gmc/installation/#install-the-gmc-plugin
